The ALB takes ~2-3 minutes after install to become reachable. Watch for
the hostname to appear:

  kubectl get ingress {{ include "microservice.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} \
    -o jsonpath='{.status.loadBalancer.ingress[0].hostname}'

Once it's set, the service is reachable at:

  curl http://<hostname>/
  curl http://<hostname>/healthz
  curl http://<hostname>/metrics

Pods:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}
